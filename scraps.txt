About Head & Heart Health

At Head & Heart Health, we bring a wealth of life experience to our practice, understanding the complexities of relationships and personal growth. Our team has navigated various personal challenges, including marriage, parenting, divorce, and blended families, and we value genuine conversations that build trust and therapeutic relationships.

We maintain strong, supportive relationships with our clients and their families, and we believe in discovering one's true calling at any stage in life. Our approach is infused with positivity and humor, often using laughter as a coping mechanism. We enjoy learning about a wide range of topics and hold firm spiritual beliefs without imposing them on others.

In our sessions, we use analogies and references from movies, books, music, and social media to connect with our clients. We focus on building rapport and trust, allowing clients to express their thoughts and feelings openly. At Head & Heart Health, we help clients explore their goals, whether they seek a different perspective, validation, or simply someone to listen.

Therapeutic Approach

Our approach is client-centered and flexible, tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. We encourage clients to share their thoughts and feelings, offering guidance when needed. Together, we explore questions about self-improvement, emotional well-being, and life's challenges.

Specialties and Interests

Trauma: Utilizing effective protocols to aid in healing and understanding individual experiences.
Dissociative Identity Disorder
Life Changes: Addressing lack of life satisfaction and questioning purpose.
Relationships: Supporting alternative lifestyles, intimacy, codependency, and domestic violence.
LGBTQIA+ Affirming Therapy
Spirituality: Exploring beliefs and addressing religious trauma.
At Head & Heart Health, we are dedicated to continual education and staying informed about the latest therapeutic practices to provide the best care for our clients.

================== FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
Do you take private insurance and state-funded insurance?
Yes! We are constantly working to be in network with as many insurance providers as we can to make therapy accessible to anyone in need.

Do you see children?
Yes! Adults aren't the only ones who can benefit from therapy. We can schedule children as young as six-years-old.

Can I do virtual visits?
Of course! We all lead busy lives. I want to meet your needs. We offer in-office visits in Greensboro, NC, but if you live in Kentucky or just can't make an in person meet, we can schedule a telehealth session through our patient portal!

What if I'm paying out of pocket?
No problem! We can take cash in-office or credit card payments in the patient portal. We also accept Venmo, Zelle, and CashApp!
