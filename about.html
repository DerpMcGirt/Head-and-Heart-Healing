Welcome to Head & Heart Healing

Your Journey to Wellness Begins Now!

At Head & Heart Healing, we bring a wealth of life experience to our practice, understanding the complexities of relationships and personal growth. Our team has navigated various personal challenges, including marriage, parenting, divorce, and blended families, and we value genuine conversations that build trust and therapeutic relationships.

We maintain strong, supportive relationships with our clients and their families, and we believe in discovering one's true calling at any stage in life. Our approach is infused with positivity and humor, often using laughter as a coping mechanism. We enjoy learning about a wide range of topics and hold firm spiritual beliefs without imposing them on others.

In our sessions, we use analogies and references from movies, books, music, and social media to connect with our clients. We focus on building rapport and trust, allowing clients to express their thoughts and feelings openly. At Head & Heart Healing, we help clients explore their goals, whether they seek a different perspective, validation, or simply someone to listen.
